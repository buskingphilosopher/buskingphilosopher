About

Welcome to The Busking Philosopher's blog— a space where street fog drifts through your bedroom window, engulfs your computer monitor and digitalises the philosophy I typewrite about.

I have a Residency every Saturday at Reference.Point where I typewrite outside on the steps and talk to passersby, if they so choose, about philosophy.

I ask them to think up any 5 words and I talk them through philosophical ideas related to those words while also typing them a page of philosophy they can walk away with.

Write to me with your own 5 words and I'll upload my response on this blog. Reach out to buskingphilosopher@gmail.com.

Thanks for visiting.
